Ingredients:
 - 1 cup of rice (sit overnight in fridge)
 - dash of soy sauce
 - salt
 - pepper
 - corn
 - carrot
 - peas
 - eggs
 - chinese bbq pork

Directions:
 1. Add oil to the frying pan, then heat frying pan with medium-high heat.
 2. Add rice into the pan, stir fry the rice and make sure each grain of rice is
    coated with oil
 3. Add beaten eggs into the rice gradually to ensure the eggs are spread evenly
    across the rice
 4. Add the remaining ingredients
 5. Profit!
